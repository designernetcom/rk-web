About ‘RK INDUSTRIES’

THE WORLD OF RK INDUSTRIES

Welcome to RK Industries, where precision meets excellence in the world of plastic injection molding components. Established with a vision to deliver top-tier products and services, RK Industries has grown to become a trusted name in the automotive and electronics sectors.

We pride ourselves on our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and innovation. Guided by our core values of responsibility, imagination, and passion, we strive to exceed customer expectations at every step.

At RK Industries, we believe in sustainable growth that benefits not just our business but also the environment and society. Our team of skilled professionals and state-of-the-art technology enables us to deliver reliable, cost-effective, and high-quality solutions tailored to your needs.

Whether it’s creating components for cutting-edge automotive applications or intricate electronics, RK Industries is your partner in excellence.

Our Company Milestones

2010

Foundation of RK Industries

RK Industries was established in Pune, Maharashtra, with a vision to provide high-quality plastic injection molding components.

2012

Expansion into the Automotive Sector

Gained recognition as a reliable supplier of precision components for the automotive industry, marking a significant milestone in our journey.

2015

Investment in Advanced Technology

Upgraded our production facility with state-of-the-art machinery, including Milacron 110T and 150T, to enhance production capabilities and meet growing demand.

2017

Commitment to Quality Standards

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification, demonstrating our commitment to delivering products that meet global quality standards.

2019

Diversification into Electronics Components

Expanded our portfolio to include precision components for the electronics sector, establishing ourselves as a trusted partner in the industry.

2021

Focus on Sustainability and Safety

Adopted environmentally friendly practices and strengthened our commitment to employee occupational health and safety.

2023

Enhanced Capabilities

Introduced new mold maintenance services, including polishing and correction for lenses, bezels, and trim panels, providing comprehensive solutions to our clients.

About Company

At RK Industries, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality precision plastic injection molding components, catering primarily to the automotive and electronics industries.
